# Incident Response Runbook

## Severity Classification
Severity 1 incidents are outages or security events with critical business impact. The incident commander must be assigned within 15 minutes of declaration.

## Containment
For suspected credential compromise, force password reset and revoke active sessions immediately. If production systems are affected, isolate impacted services before forensic imaging.

## Communication
Stakeholder updates must be sent every 30 minutes for Severity 1 incidents until service is restored. A post-incident review must be completed within five business days.
