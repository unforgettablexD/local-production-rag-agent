# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Submission Window
Employees must submit reimbursable expenses within 30 calendar days of the purchase date. Expenses submitted after 30 days require director approval and may be denied.

## Travel Limits
Hotel reimbursements are capped at 250 USD per night before tax unless a conference hotel is pre-approved. Airfare should be economy class for flights under six hours.

## Receipts
Receipts are required for any single expense of 25 USD or more. Missing receipt affidavits may only be used twice per quarter.
