# Security Policy

## Access Control
All employees and contractors must use multi-factor authentication on company-managed systems. Passwords must be at least 14 characters long and may not be reused across personal and corporate accounts.

## Device Management
Laptops must enable full-disk encryption and screen lock after 10 minutes of inactivity. Security patches rated critical must be applied within 72 hours.

## Data Handling
Confidential customer data may only be stored in approved systems. Copying production data to local personal devices is prohibited.
