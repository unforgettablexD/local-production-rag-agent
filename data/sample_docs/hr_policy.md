# Human Resources Policy

## Paid Time Off
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid time off per calendar year during their first three years of employment. Starting in year four, accrual increases to 20 days annually. PTO requests longer than three consecutive business days require manager approval in the HR portal.

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Roles classified as customer-facing operations must maintain at least two days per week onsite unless an exception is approved by the HR director.

## Leave of Absence
Employees must notify their manager and HR at least 30 days before planned leave when practical. Medical leave documentation must be submitted within 10 business days of the leave start date.
